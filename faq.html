Frequently Asked Questions

What is ThinkForge?

ThinkForge is a crowdfunding platform specifically designed for student projects and initiatives. We help connect innovative student ideas with potential supporters and resources.

How do I start a campaign?

To start a campaign:

  1. Click on "Start Campaign" button
  2. Fill in your project details
  3. Verify your school/college affiliation
  4. Submit for review

How do donations work?

Donations are processed securely through our platform. You can support projects using credit/debit cards or other payment methods. Funds are released to campaign creators after verification and project milestones.

How do I verify my student status?

Student verification requires:

  • Valid institutional email address
  • Current student/faculty ID
  • Brief description of your role
