About This Project

The Tech Innovation Hub is a student-led initiative aimed at creating a state-of-the-art technology workspace at our university. This space will serve as a catalyst for innovation, learning, and collaboration among students from various disciplines.

Project Goals

  • Create a modern tech workspace with 24/7 access
  • Provide access to latest equipment including 3D printers and VR setup
  • Foster innovation and collaboration through workshops
  • Support student startups and research projects
