About This Project

The Student Research Fund aims to empower undergraduate students by providing financial support for innovative research projects across all disciplines. This initiative will enable students to pursue groundbreaking research and contribute to their fields of study while gaining valuable experience.

Project Goals

  • Provide advanced research equipment
