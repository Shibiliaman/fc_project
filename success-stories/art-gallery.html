Completed: February 2025 180+ Backers ₹12,00,000 Raised

The Journey

What started as a dream to provide student artists with a professional exhibition space turned into a vibrant cultural hub on campus. The Art Gallery project brought together talented artists, art enthusiasts, and the local community.

Impact & Results

  • Showcased works of 50+ student artists
  • Created partnerships with local galleries
  • Generated ₹3,00,000 in art sales for students
  • Established annual exhibition program
"The gallery gave us a platform to showcase our work professionally and connect with the art community. It's more than just a space - it's where dreams take shape."
- Maya Shah, Student Artist
